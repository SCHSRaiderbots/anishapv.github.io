                                  OUR MISSION

SCHS Raiderbots, the official Robotics Team of Silver Creek High School, is a student-led organization that enables students to explore STEM pathways through hands-on mechanical and software design and development. Guided by our amazing coaches and mentors, we learn and practice engineering principles and techniques over the course of designing, building, and programming our competition-ready robot. Founded on principles of dedication, innovation, and collaboration, our team is open to all Silver Creek High School Students, regardless of prior experience.

 

                          SCHS Raiderbots

Silver Creek High School Robotics is a FIRST Robotics Team founded in 2018. The SCHS Raiderbots, also known as FTC Team 15453, are a dedicated group of students passionate about technology and design. We are supported by a community of mentors, teachers, and parents committed to the success of our fledgling team as we begin our FTC journey.

              Silver Creek High School

Founded in 1969 in San Jose, Silver Creek High School is a California Distinguished School and the home of the SCHS Raiderbots. As a student-run club, our team embraces Silver Creek High's mission to provide all students with equal access to quality education and opportunity in a supportive, encouraging environment.
