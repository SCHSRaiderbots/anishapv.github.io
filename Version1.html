px;text-align:center>                                    Our Mission

elvetica;font-size:20px">​​SCHS Raiderbots, the official Robotics Team of Silver Creek High School, is a student-led organization that enables students to explore STEM pathways through hands-on mechanical and software design and development. Guided by our amazing coaches and mentors, we learn and practice engineering principles and techniques over the course of designing, building, and programming our competition-ready robot. Founded on principles of dedication, innovation, and collaboration, our team is open to all Silver Creek High School Students, regardless of prior experience.
